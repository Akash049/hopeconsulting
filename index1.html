Meet Your Counsellor

Reshma — Warm, Respectful
and Non-Judgemental Support

“Whatever your reason for seeking therapy, I believe it should be an empowering process.”

I offer a safe and confidential space where you can learn more about yourself and the person you would like to become. You can expect me to put you first, to be understanding, respectful, and non-judgemental.

I understand that therapy can be a difficult emotional experience. That is why I work with you, at your pace, helping you find your own answers and make the changes — externally or within yourself — that allow you to move forward.

I am professionally qualified and BACP registered, providing one-to-one counselling for adults and young people. I am trained in the Person-Centred Approach and integrate elements of other modalities and resources where they can support your individual needs.

My work is shaped by more than twenty years of therapeutic experience with children and families, including support around child protection cases, trauma, conditioning, and patterns that shape lives at a deep level. Alongside private practice, I also work with a domestic abuse charity, offering one-to-one counselling and group work.

Common Questions

Things You May Want to Know

We understand you may have questions. Here are answers to some of the most common ones. Please don't hesitate to reach out if you need more information.

How long does a therapy session last?
+
A standard therapy session is 50 minutes. Group and supervision sessions may be extended to 75 or 90 minutes. We will always agree on the duration before your first session.
Is everything I share kept confidential?
+
Yes. All sessions are strictly confidential in line with BACP ethical guidelines. The only exceptions are if there is a risk of serious harm to yourself or others, in which case your therapist will discuss this with you first wherever possible.
How much does a session cost?
+
Session fees typically range from £60–£120 per session depending on the type of therapy and the therapist. We also offer a sliding scale for those on lower incomes. Please contact us for more details.
Do you offer a free initial consultation?
+
Yes, we offer a complimentary 20-minute telephone or video consultation so you can get a sense of how we work and ask any questions before committing to a full session.
How many sessions will I need?
+
This varies for each person and depends on your goals. Some clients benefit from 6–12 sessions; others prefer longer-term work. We review progress regularly and always work at your pace.
Can I access therapy if I live outside a major city?
+
Absolutely. Our online and telephone therapy services are available to clients across the whole of the UK, regardless of location.
